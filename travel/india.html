Taj Mahal

Day 1: 10AM-2PM

The Taj Mahal is an iconic white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. The Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and considered one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Visiting this location in the morning is advised and a personal guide will be offered to each group.


1. It is advised to wear decent ethnic outfits keeping in mind the culture of India
2. Carry a personal camera for photography in allowed areas

Agra Fort

Day 1: 3PM-5PM

The Agra Fort is a historical fort in the city of Agra. It was the main residence of the emperors of the Mughal Dynasty until 1638 when the capital was shifted from Agra to Delhi. The fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Fatehpur Sikri

Day 1: 6PM

Fatehpur Sikri is a city in the Agra district of Uttar Pradesh, India. It was built by the Mughal Emperor Akbar in the 16th century and served as the capital of the Mughal Empire from 1571 to 1585. The city is known for its well-preserved architecture and historical significance.
